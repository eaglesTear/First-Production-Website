Anjuli Clare McKenna

Illustrator and Graphic Designer


The very definition of a Virgo ♍, I am a designer, image maker and crafter based in Birmingham, England.


I have developed a sleek visual language that translates across branding, design and illustration. Having a degree in illustration and spending some time working in agencies as a graphic designer, it has taken me years to realise I am a designer who illustrates...Or is that an illustrator who designs?


My line work is a defining characteristic of all my work and I enjoy a clean and fresh aesthetic. My work comes in the form of confined spaces and uses the guide of crests and heraldry. Hugely influenced by Scandinavian design and coming from a house with a rich range of cultural references, my work takes on many different influences.


More recently, I have been commissioned to design commemorative wedding crests as presents, which are available for sale in my shop.
